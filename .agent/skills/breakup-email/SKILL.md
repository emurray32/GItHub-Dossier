# Breakup Email (Email 4 — Final)

This is the last email. Its job is NOT to close a deal. Its job is to:
1. Respectfully close the loop
2. Leave the door open for future timing
3. Get a "not now" reply (any reply is better than silence)

## The Formula

```
Hey {{first_name}},

[One sentence acknowledging you're closing the loop.]

[One sentence leaving the door open.]

{{sender_first_name}}
```

That's it. 2 sentences. Under 40 words for the body.

## Approved Breakup Templates

### Template A: The Clean Close
```
Hey {{first_name}},

Just want to make sure I'm not cluttering your inbox. If localization tooling isn't on the radar right now, no worries at all.

Here whenever timing is right.

{{sender_first_name}}
```

### Template B: The Permission to Say No
```
Hey {{first_name}},

Totally understand if this isn't a priority right now. Happy to reconnect down the road if i18n moves up the list.

{{sender_first_name}}
```

### Template C: The One-Liner
```
Hey {{first_name}},

Should I close the loop on this, or is it worth revisiting next quarter?

{{sender_first_name}}
```

### Template D: The Graceful Exit
```
Hey {{first_name}},

Last note from me on this. If internationalization comes back onto the roadmap, I'm an easy ping away.

{{sender_first_name}}
```

## Breakup Email Rules

### DO:
- Keep it under 40 words (body only)
- Acknowledge that you're stopping outreach
- Leave ONE clear path to re-engage ("here whenever," "easy ping away")
- Use a question format if possible (questions get replies)
- Sound genuinely human — this is where sincerity matters most

### DO NOT:
- Guilt trip ("I've sent several emails...")
- Fake scarcity ("This offer expires...")
- Passive aggression ("Since I haven't heard back...")
- Summarize all previous emails
- Re-pitch Phrase features
- Use "just" more than once
- Include any links or resources
- Add a PS ("PS — we just launched...")

## Subject Lines for Breakup
- "Closing the loop — {{company}}"
- "Quick question — {{company}}"
- "Should I close this out?"
- "One last thought — {{company}}"

## Why Breakup Emails Work
Breakup emails have the highest reply rate in most sequences (10-15% vs. 3-5% for Email 1). People reply to permission to say no. A "not right now" reply is valuable data — it confirms the contact is real, engaged, and can be re-sequenced in 3-6 months.

## Timing
- Email 3 → Email 4: 5-7 business days
- After Email 4 with no reply: Wait 90 days before re-sequencing
